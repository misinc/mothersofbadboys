What I'm good at

Saturday, October 17, 2009

This is a homework assignment by Ben in fifth grade. The instruction was to write a couple of paragraphs on what he thinks he is good at, in response to this quote from President Obama’s recent address to students:


Every single one of you has something you’re good at. Every single one of you has something to offer. And you have a responsibility to yourself to discover what that is. That’s the opportunity an education can provide.


Sleeping


Sleeping is one of my specialties. In fact it is my only specialty. What makes me fall asleep is a good massage. You are probably wondering what sleeping has to do with education? Well, when I’m being educated, I fall asleep, like now ZZZZZZ.


AAA…! I’m awake, I’m awake! Jeez, don’t do that. Now that you scared me awake I won’t write any more. HMF!
